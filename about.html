My Journey

I'm Daniel Wanjala, a passionate Data Scientist and Machine Learning Engineer with a unique journey that bridges traditional IT management and cutting-edge analytics. My career transformation from managing IT systems to building predictive models represents my commitment to continuous learning and innovation.

With a strong foundation in statistical analysis, programming, and business intelligence, I specialize in transforming complex datasets into actionable insights that drive strategic decision-making. My approach combines technical expertise with business acumen to deliver solutions that create real value.

When I'm not analyzing data or building models, you'll find me exploring new machine learning techniques, contributing to open-source projects, or mentoring aspiring data scientists. I believe in the power of data to solve real-world problems and improve lives.
