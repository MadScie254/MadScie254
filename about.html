My Journey in Data Science

From IT management to advanced analytics - discover the path that led me to become a passionate data scientist and machine learning engineer.

Who I Am

I'm Daniel Wanjala, a Senior IT Manager turned Data Scientist, based near Nairobi, Kenya. My unique journey combines hands-on IT leadership with advanced data science expertise, giving me a rare perspective on how technology can transform organizations from the ground up.

At Kingdom Hospital, I wear multiple hats - managing IT infrastructure while pioneering data-driven solutions as a volunteer Data Scientist. This dual role has taught me that the most impactful insights come from understanding both the technical foundation and the business challenges that data can solve.

My passion lies in predictive analytics and machine learning applications that create tangible value. Whether it's optimizing hospital operations, forecasting demand, or building intelligent systems, I thrive on turning complex data into actionable solutions.
